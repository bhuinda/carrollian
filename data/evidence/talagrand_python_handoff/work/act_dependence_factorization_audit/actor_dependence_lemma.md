# Actor Dependence Lemma

Let \(C_2\) be the scenic face space and let

\[
Act:C_2\to\mathcal P_3
\]

send a face scene to its public three-level shell profile.

For the audited scenic toric complex, the Talagrand shell readout on face residues factors through \(Act\). Consequently every audited actor-visible \(H_2\) residue is a finite sum of certified three-level shell gaps, hence nonnegative.

This lemma is certified only for the audited rest-size \(\le 6\) band.
